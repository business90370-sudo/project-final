Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common loan questions.

How fast will I get the money?

It depends on the lender, but many users get approval within 1 hour and payout the same day.

Do you charge any fee to apply?

No. Browsing offers and applying is free. Any charges (if applicable) are shown by the lender clearly.

What documents do I need?

Usually basic ID proof and bank details. Some lenders may ask for income proof depending on the amount.

Can I repay early?

Many lenders allow early repayment. Check the offer terms to see if there is any early-closure charge.

Is my data safe?

We only share your details with the lender you choose. Always read the privacy policy before submitting.
